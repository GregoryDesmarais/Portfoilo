About Me

Good Morning/Afternoon/Evening, and thank you for taking the time to look at my webpage! I have been in the Information Technology field for over 10 years, from installing structured cable, to building servers, and developing useful web tools.

Full Stack Web Developer with several years of background in Data Centers as a Technician. During this time, I created processes and web-based tools that increased productivity of the Network Operations Center.

TicketWatcher, a new front-end for a ticketing system, which provided both visual and audible alerts, a complex filter, including, but not limited to teams, owner, customer name, and ticket status. This enabled a faster customer response time, which improved customer satisfaction.

Another project was a site which centralized the environmental sensor data, which allowed for at-a-glance view for zone temperatures, as well as visual/audible alerts. This allowed the team to stay on top of data center temperatures in-between regular data center walkthroughs.

After developing these tools, I was referred to a boot camp course by a friend that had recently completed it. I decided to take this course to polish the skills I have learned to professional levels. In January 2020, I have earned a certificate in Full Stack Development from UNC Charlotte.
